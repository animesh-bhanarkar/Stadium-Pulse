🚨 Emergency & Evacuation Plan

In the event of a stadium-wide evacuation command, stay calm and comply with these instructions:

  • Identify the nearest lit emergency green exit sign closest to your row.
  • Do NOT use elevators. Use concrete stairwells and ramps exclusively.
  • Follow guidelines shouted by yellow-vested volunteers and stewards.
  • Proceed directly to designated Zone assembly fields outside gates.

🔍 Lost & Found Reclamation Center

Reclaiming missing belongings or reporting family separation details:

  • The Central Guest Relations desk is located at Level 1, Concourse Area (near Sector 104).
  • Missing items are photographed and logged electronically. Reclaim with ticketing/ID proof.
  • For lost children reports: Notify any staff instantly to broadcast local zone descriptions.

🏥 First-Aid & Medical Care Centers

First-aid response teams operate stations in the following sectors:

  • Station 1 (North): Behind Sector 108, Lower Concourse.
  • Station 2 (South): Behind Sector 132, Lower Concourse.
  • Station 3 (Upper): Adjacent to Sector 215, Level 2.
  • Paramedics patrol concourses on foot and with carts. Wave them down for rapid aid.
